Thus combining word2vec and co-occurrence statistics in a single model</span></p><p class="c0 c34 c26 c44"><span class="c6">GloVe is to learn word vectors such that their dot product equals the logarithm of the words&#39; probability of co-occurrence. Owing to the fact that the logarithm of a ratio equals the difference of logarithms, this objective associates (the logarithm of) ratios of co-occurrence probabilities with vector differences in the word vector space. Because these ratios can encode some form of meaning, this information gets encoded as vector differences as well.
